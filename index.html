6-Month CSE Study Schedule

This schedule is designed for a CSE student, balancing academic rigor with personal time. It assumes a 6-month semester with daily classes.

2:00 PM - 2:30 PM Travel from College 30 Minutes
2:30 PM - 3:00 PM Lunch 30 Minutes

5:00 PM - 5:30 PM Snacks 30 Minutes
5:30 PM - 7:30 PM Fun Time (Games, Hobbies, Relaxation) 2 Hours

10:00 PM Onwards Relax/Sleep Flexible
